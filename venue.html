ABOUT BAGHDAD FAIR:

THE STATE COMPANY FOR IRAQI FAIRS AND COMMERCIAL SERVICES IS A SELF-FINANCED ECONOMIC UNIT ENJOYING MORAL PERSONALITY

AND FINANCIAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE INDEPENDENCE. IT IS A RESULT OF ITS MERGE WITH THE STATE COMPANY FOR IMPORT AND EXPORT

ACCORDING TO THE DECISION OF THE COUNCIL OF MINISTERS NO. 152 IN 2010.

THE COMPANY IS ONE OF THE MINISTRY OF TRADE’S FORMATIONS.
